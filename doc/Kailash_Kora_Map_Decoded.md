## 1 · Overall route summary  (转山总览)

| Item | Value |
|---|---|
| Total outer kora distance | ~**50 km** (main pilgrim path only, excluding side-monastery detours) |
| Plan | **3 stages over 2½ days** (recommended for pilgrims of average fitness) |
| Stage I (蓝线 / blue) — **D1** | 塔钦 → 岗加/芝热寺 · ~20 km · **7–8 hrs** (起点 4690 m → 5060/5085 m) |
| Stage II (红线 / red) — **D2** | 岗加/芝热寺 → 卓玛拉垭口 → 尊珠寺 · ~20 km · **10–11 hrs** (max 5650 m) |
| Stage III (绿线 / green) — **D3 (half-day)** | 尊珠寺 → 塔钦 · 10.6 km · **3.5–4 hrs** |
| Pacing basis | Unhurried, safe, comfortable pace — longer than typical "outdoor hiker" itineraries |

Each stage's published duration includes reasonable rest, meal/supply, photo, and prostration time; individual sub-segment times below cover only walking + short pauses.

## 2 · Stage I (I 蓝线) — D1 塔钦 → 岗加/芝热寺

Start: **塔钦 Tarchen 4690 m** · Finish: **岗加/芝热寺 Gangga / Drira-puk Monastery 5060 m (lodge) / 5085 m (monastery)** · 1st-night accommodation point.

| Seg | From → To | Distance | Time | Profile (Chinese → English) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **I-1** | 塔钦 → 第一朝拜处 (1st Prostration Point 4870 m) | 3.8 / 4 km | 1 hr | 较缓的持续上坡 — *gentle continuous ascent* |
| **I-2** | 第一朝拜处 → 色雄 Sershong (supply pt w/ food & lodging 5020 m) | 2.3 km | 30 min | 下坡及平路为主 — *mostly descent and level* |
| **I-3** | 色雄 → 曲古桥补给点 Chuku-Bridge supply (4830 m) | 2.4 km | 40 min | 平路为主 — *mostly flat* |
| **I-4** | 曲古桥补给点 → 第二朝拜处 (2nd Prostration Point 4900 m, 医疗服务点 medical post) | 5.5 km | 100 min | 逐渐上升中有多段起伏 — *gradual ascent with rolling sections* |
| **I-5** | 第二朝拜处 → 马头明王驿站 Hayagrīva Station (4900 m supply pt) | 1.5 km | 30 min | 轻微上升中有起伏 — *slight ascent with undulations* |
| **I-6** | 马头明王驿站 → 岗加/芝热寺 (5060 / 5085 m, **第一天住宿点** 1st-night lodging) | 4.3 / 4.7 km | 1.5 hr | 逐渐上升中略有起伏 — *gradual ascent with slight undulations* |

**Stage I notable in-path sights**
- 第一朝拜处 (1st Prostration Point) — first full view of Mt. Kailash's south face.
- 色雄 Sershong — large supply / lodging cluster at the open plain.
- 大天葬台 / 五百罗汉天葬台 (Big Sky-Burial Platform / "500 Arhats" sky-burial) · 大塔轮回 (Tarboche / 达波切 / 经幡广场 prayer-flag pole plaza) · 双腿佛塔 (Two-legged Stupa).
- 曲古寺 Chuku Monastery (4870 m).
- 第二朝拜处 with 西库桑姆神山 (Sekula 旦真仲塘 supply point, 4900 m).
- 自生马头明王像 · 自生龙女、蛇头佛像 (玻姆石) — self-arisen Hayagrīva and Nāga / serpent-head images on "Bomu Stone".
- 格萨尔马鞍鞍 (Gesar's saddle).
- 岗加/芝热寺 Drira-puk Gompa — first-night dormitory village.

## 3 · Stage II (II 红线) — D2 岗加/芝热寺 → 卓玛拉垭口 → 尊珠寺

Start: **岗加/芝热寺 5060 m** · Crosses **卓玛拉垭口 Drölma-la Pass 5650 m** · Finish: **尊珠寺 Zutul-puk Monastery 4910 m (lodge) / 4830 m (monastery)** · 2nd-night accommodation point.

| Seg | From → To | Distance | Time | Profile |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **II-1** | 岗加/芝热寺 → 小天葬台西侧补给点 (West-of-Small-Sky-Burial supply 5290 m) | 2.1 / 2.4 km | 80 min | 陡坡 + 缓坡 — *steep then gentle* |
| **II-2** | 小天葬台西侧补给点 → 开始爬卓玛拉处 (Start-of-Drölma-La-climb 5480 m) | 2.5 km | 1.5 hr | 缓坡接小段陡坡后大段缓坡 — *gentle → short steep → long gentle* |
| **II-3** | 开始爬卓玛拉处 → 卓玛拉垭口 Drölma-La Pass 5650 m ⚠ 注意安全 | 1.4 km | 1.5 hr | 先陡坡后缓坡 — *steep first, then gentle* |
| **II-4** | 卓玛拉垭口 → 不动地钉补给点 Achala-Earth-Nail supply 5240 m ⚠ 注意安全 | 3 km | 1.5 hr | 下山陡坡及缓坡 — *descent: steep & gentle. Watch your footing!* |
| **II-5** | 不动地钉补给点 → 药师佛沟补给点 Bhaiṣajya-guru-Ravine (桑杰曼拉) 4970 m | 5.7 km | 100 min | 整体缓慢下坡有多段起伏 — *overall gentle descent with rolling* |
| **II-6** | 药师佛沟补给点 → 尊珠寺 4910 m (**第二天住宿点** 2nd-night lodging) | 4.6 km | 80 min | 整体缓慢下坡有多段起伏 — *overall gentle descent with rolling* |

**Stage II notable in-path sights**
- 狼脚爪印 · 鸟爪爪印 (Wolf-paw and bird-claw imprints in rock).
- 小天葬台 (Small Sky-Burial Platform).
- 第三朝拜处 (3rd Prostration Point 5150 m) at 坡龙古补给点 (Polong-gu supply pt) near the steep section.
- 卓玛拉垭口 — Tāra Pass (5650 m), highest point of the kora; crossing it is said to absolve a lifetime of sin.
- 不动地钉 (Achala "Earth-Nail" Stupa) just below the pass.
- 慈悲湖 / 圣湖 (Lake of Compassion / Gauri-kund) lies just south of the pass (visible in the photo strip).
- 药师佛沟 / 桑杰曼拉 (Medicine-Buddha Ravine).
- 喇玛巴黑娟宝冠印记 (Lama Bahei-juan / Karmapa Crown imprint stone) near 尊珠寺.
- 尊珠寺 Zutul-puk Gompa — second-night dormitory village.

## 4 · Stage III (III 绿线) — D3 (half-day) 尊珠寺 → 塔钦

Start: **尊珠寺 4910 m** · Finish: **塔钦 Tarchen 4690 m** (kora terminus = original start).

| Seg | From → To | Distance | Time | Profile |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **III-1** | 尊珠寺 → 宗堆补给点 Zongdui supply (4830 m) | 6.7 km | 2 hr | 整体缓慢下降有多段起伏 — *overall gentle descent w/ rolling* |
| **III-2** | 宗堆补给点 → 塔钦 (4690 m) | 3.9 km | 1 hr | 先降后升有多段小起伏 — *descend first, then ascend, small rolling* |

**Stage III notable in-path sights**
- 根敦拉曲 (Gendün-la-chu / stream).
- 梨壁巨石 (Pear-wall boulder).
- 空行母浴场 (Ḍākinī Bathing Pool).
- 第四朝拜处 (4th Prostration Point 4700 m) — final view of Kailash before re-entering Tarchen.
- 黄绿色岩段 (yellow-green rock band) just before the finish.
