## Religious significance summary

**Chinese:** 冈仁波齐是本教（苯教）、印度教、耆那教、藏传佛教四个古老宗教共同认定的圣山，藏语名意为「珍贵的雪山」。在藏地众多神山中位居其首，具有无上的宗教地位。

**English:** Mount Kailash is the sacred mountain jointly venerated by four ancient religions: Bon, Hinduism, Jainism, and Tibetan Buddhism. Its Tibetan name means 'precious snow mountain'. It holds the highest religious status among all sacred mountains of Tibet.
